Note: physical pin locations may only be accurate for Raspberry Pi 5

Note: motors A (front) and C (back) are on right side <<from top down view>>.
Motor A wire 1 → AO1
Motor A wire 2 → AO2
Motor C wire 1 → AO1
Motor C wire 2 → AO2

Note: motors B (front) and D (back) are on left side <<from top down view>>.
Motor B wire 1 → BO1
Motor B wire 2 → BO2
Motor D wire 1 → BO1
Motor D wire 2 → BO2

Pi 3.3V, physical pin 1 → VCC
Pi GND, physical pin 6  → GND
Pi GND + battery black + TB6612FNG GND all connect together

Battery (+) red wire   → VM
Battery (-) black wire → GND

STBY → Pi GPIO22, physical pin 15

PWMA → Pi GPIO18, physical pin 12
AIN2 → Pi GPIO27, physical pin 13
AIN1 → Pi GPIO17, physical pin 11


BIN1 → Pi GPIO23, physical pin 16
BIN2 → Pi GPIO24, physical pin 18
PWMB → Pi GPIO13, physical pin 33
